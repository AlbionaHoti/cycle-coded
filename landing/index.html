runs on your computer

There is no cycle-coded server for your health data. Optional MCP stores cycle state only in ~/.cycle-coded/ on your machine. Import scripts read Apple Health exports locally — they don’t upload. No accounts. No analytics. Clear anytime. (If you paste dates into Claude chat, that follows Anthropic’s policy — cycle-coded doesn’t add a second upload path.)
