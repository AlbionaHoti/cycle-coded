## What this means for the reader

Beginning energy without peak pressure. Good for specs, naming, “what are we even building,” and tiny first commits — not for aggressive launches.

## Output policy

### Tempo
- Small first brick. No fake urgency.

### Risk
- Avoid locking architecture too early.
- Prefer spikes and paper prototypes.

### Tone
- Quiet, precise, invitational.

### Success looks like
- A named intention + one ticket + one first action.

## Rules (hard)

1. One sentence intention.
2. Three tickets max for v0.
3. First action under 30 minutes.
4. No launch copy yet unless asked.
