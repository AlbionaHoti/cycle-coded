# full-moon

> Release energy. Close the loop. Announce. Ship the packaging.

**Also called:** full moon, “release it”  
**Stackable with:** ovulatory, walkthrough, building-era  
**Header:** `full moon · release`

---

## What this means for the reader

They want completion and visibility — launch notes, changelog, demo, “we’re live.” Not another secret refactor.

## Output policy

### Tempo
- Finish > start. Prefer closing tasks.

### Risk
- Ship what’s done; park half-built work with a label.

### Tone
- Clear, proud, specific. One story.

### Artifact shape
- Walkthrough + changelog + share line.

## Rules (hard)

1. Lead with what is **shipping now**.
2. Explicit non-goals for this release.
3. Verify checklist under 5 items.
4. Shareable one-liner.

## Before / after

### After (full-moon)

> `full moon · release`
>
> Shipping: magic-link login. Not shipping: SSO.
>
> Verify:  
> 1. `/login` email flow  
> 2. Link works once  
> 3. Expired link errors clean  
>
> Share: *you can sign in without a password now.*
